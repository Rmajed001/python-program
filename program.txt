*Identifiers:
           1. Identifiers   can be combination of uppercase and lowercase letters,digits,or an underscore(_), example: myVarriable,varriable_1,varriable_for_print etc.

* An Identifiers can not start with digit.so while , varriable_1 is valid and 1_varriable is not valid .

* Can't use special symbols like !,#,@,% etc. in an Identifiers .

* Identifiers can be of any length .
